## CONSENT FOR TELEHEALTH CONSULTATIONS

### Purpose
This form authorizes your healthcare clinic to use secure telehealth (video conferencing) for consultations and follow-up appointments.

### Telehealth Features
Your clinic can:
1. 📹 Generate secure video call links
2. 🔗 Send links via email or SMS
3. 💬 Conduct video consultations
4. 📝 Document visit notes in your chart
5. 💊 Send prescriptions electronically

### Benefits of Telehealth
- 🏠 **Visit from home:** No travel needed
- ⏱️ **Flexible scheduling:** Extended hours
- 👨‍⚕️ **Same provider:** Consistent care
- 📹 **Face-to-face:** Real-time interaction
- 🔐 **Secure:** Encrypted video connection

### Privacy & Security
- ✅ Video calls are **HIPAA-encrypted**
- ✅ Links are **one-time use** with security token
- ✅ No recording without your consent
- ✅ Sessions logged for compliance
- ✅ Chat messages are protected

### Your Responsibilities
- 📱 **Reliable device:** Computer or smartphone
- 📡 **Good internet:** WiFi recommended
- 🔒 **Private space:** No one overhearing
- 🪪 **Valid ID:** Verify before visit
- 📋 **Background info:** Prepare questions

### Not Appropriate for Telehealth
Telehealth is **NOT** suitable for:
- ❌ Emergencies (call 911)
- ❌ In-person exams needed (blood pressure, physical)
- ❌ Complex procedures (injections, suturing)
- ❌ Severe psychiatric symptoms
- ❌ High-risk medications requiring monitoring

### Consent to Telehealth
**I understand telehealth and authorize video consultations:**
- ☐ YES, I consent to telehealth
- ☐ NO, I prefer in-person only
