🔐 Logging In & Role-Based Access All Roles

1
Navigate to Clarity

Open your browser and go to your practice's Clarity URL (e.g., app.clarity.health/Clarity).

2
Enter Credentials

Type your username (email or NPI) and password. Click Sign In.

3
Two-Factor Auth (if enabled)

Enter the 6-digit OTP sent to your registered device. Codes expire in 30 seconds.

4
Redirected to Dashboard

You land on the Dashboard filtered to your role. Patient portal users are redirected to the Patient Portal.

👥 Available Roles
Prescriber (MD/DO/NP/PA)Full clinical + prescribing access
Therapist / CounselorClinical notes, no prescribing
Nurse / MAVitals, labs, refill routing
Front DeskScheduling, check-in, billing
AdministratorAll sections + audit access
Session Timeout: Clarity auto-logs out after 15 minutes of inactivity to protect PHI. Save your work frequently.

Patient ManagementPage 4

🔍 Searching & Selecting Patients All Roles

1
Go to Patient Search

Click Patient Search in the sidebar or press Ctrl+F.

2
Type Name or MRN

Search by first name, last name, date of birth, or MRN. Results filter in real-time.

3
Select Patient

Click a result to open the patient. Their name appears in Open Charts at the top of the sidebar.

4
Multi-Chart Mode

Up to 4 charts can be open simultaneously. Click any name in the sidebar to switch.

🕐 Recent Patients

The sidebar shows your recently viewed patients for quick one-click access without searching again.

📌 Patient Banner

Once a patient is active, a persistent banner shows name, DOB, MRN, insurance, and allergies on every page — so context never gets lost.

Clinical DocumentationPage 5

🗒️ Writing Encounter Notes Prescriber Therapist Nurse

1
Open Encounters Tab

Select a patient → click Encounters in the chart sidebar. A session timer auto-starts.

2
Select Note Type

Choose from SOAP Note, Progress Note, Psychiatric Evaluation, Therapy Session Note, or custom templates.

3
Fill SOAP Sections

Subjective — chief complaint in patient's words.
Objective — vitals, exam findings.
Assessment — diagnoses (ICD-10 inline search).
Plan — medications, orders, follow-up.

4
Add Diagnoses & Orders

Search and attach ICD-10 codes. Create labs, referrals, or imaging orders directly from the encounter.

5
Sign & Lock

Click Sign Note to finalise. Locked notes are read-only and timestamped. Use Addendum to add corrections.

⚡ Smart Phrases in Notes

Type .hpi, .ros, .pe, etc. and press Tab to expand pre-built templates. Create custom phrases in Smart Phrases.

🎙️ Ambient AI Scribe

Enable in Settings → AI Features. During an encounter, click the microphone to record the conversation. Clarity's AI auto-drafts the SOAP note for review.

💡 AI Code Suggestions

After completing the assessment section, Clarity can suggest ICD-10 and CPT codes based on your note text. Click a suggestion to accept it.

🔒 Secure Notes (Sensitive Records) Prescriber Therapist

Sensitive clinical notes (e.g., substance use, HIV, psychotherapy process notes) can be stored as Secure Notes — accessible only by the treating provider. Navigate to Clinical Tools → Secure Notes.

📋 Treatment Plans Prescriber Therapist

1
Navigate to Treatment Plans

Go to Clinical Tools → Treatment Plans.

2
Create a New Plan

Click + New Plan. Select the patient, diagnosis, and target dates.

3
Add Goals & Objectives

Add measurable short-term and long-term goals. Use the progress slider to update goal completion over time.

4
Review & Update

Treatment plans carry a mandatory review date. Clarity alerts you when reviews are due.

📊 Clinical Assessments Prescriber Therapist

PHQ-9

Depression severity. Score 0–27. Alerts for suicidality (item 9 ≥ 1).

GAD-7

Generalised anxiety. Score 0–21. Colour-coded severity levels.

PCL-5

PTSD Checklist. Score 0–80. DSM-5 aligned symptom clusters.

AUDIT-C

Alcohol use screening. Quick 3-item version for annual wellness.

All scores are automatically saved to the patient chart and trend over time. Administer from Chart → Assessments.

E-Prescribing & TelehealthPage 6

💊 E-Prescribing Prescriber

1
Open E-Prescribe

Go to Clinical Tools → E-Prescribe or open a patient chart and click New Prescription.

2
Search Medication

Type the drug name. Clarity searches the live RxNorm database. Select the brand or generic.

3
Set Dose, SIG & Refills

Choose dosage form, strength, frequency (SIG), quantity, and number of refills. Add pharmacy instructions in the notes field.

4
Select Pharmacy

Search the pharmacy directory by name or zip code. Patient's preferred pharmacy auto-fills if set.

5
Send or EPCS Sign

For non-controlled drugs: click Send Prescription. For controlled substances: complete EPCS two-factor authentication (see below).

🔐 EPCS — Controlled Substances

Electronic prescribing of controlled substances requires DEA two-factor authentication:

1
Enter EPCS PIN

Your 4-digit personal DEA PIN.

2
Enter OTP

6-digit one-time code from your authenticator app. Expires in 30 sec.

3
Confirm & Send

Prescription is transmitted to pharmacy with full DEA audit trail.

🔄 Refill Requests (Therapist / Nurse)

Non-prescribers can route refill requests to the supervising prescriber via the Refill Requests queue. The prescriber reviews and approves within Clarity.

Controlled Substance Warning: Schedule II prescriptions cannot be refilled. A new prescription must be written for each fill. Clarity enforces this automatically and prevents refill fields for Schedule II drugs.

📹 Telehealth — 1:1 Video Visit All Clinical Roles

1
Start from Schedule

On the Dashboard or Schedule, find the appointment and click ▶ Start Visit. This also checks in the patient.

2
Go-To Session Link

Alternatively, use Clinical Tools → Telehealth and enter the appointment ID for a direct launch.

3
In-Session Tools

Use Chat, Screen Share, Record, and Mute controls from the session toolbar.

4
End & Document

Click End Session. You are redirected to the encounter note for the visit.

👥 Group Telehealth

Navigate to Clinical Tools → Group Telehealth. Create a session, add participants (up to 20), and launch. Breakout rooms can be created during the session for individual check-ins.

🔗 Patient Link

Patients receive a join link via SMS/email at appointment time. No app download required — works in any modern browser. The Patient Portal also has a Join Visit button.

Labs · AI Features · ReferralsPage 7

🔬 Lab Orders & Results Prescriber Nurse

Ordering Labs

1
Chart → Orders Tab

Select the patient and go to the Orders tab. Click + New Order.

2
Choose Order Type

Select Lab, Imaging, or Referral. Search for the test by name or CPT code.

3
Set Priority & Notes

Mark Routine, Urgent, or STAT. Add clinical indication and special instructions.

4
Print or Transmit

Click 🖨️ Print to print the order form or transmit electronically to an integrated lab.

Viewing Results

Inbound results appear in the Clinical Inbox and in the patient's Lab Results tab. Abnormal values are flagged in red (High) or blue (Low).

📊 Lab Tracking Dashboard

Go to Clinical Tools → Lab Tracking for a cross-patient view of all pending, resulted, and critical labs. Filter by status, urgency, or provider.

🔄 Referrals Prescriber Nurse

Navigate to Clinical Tools → Referrals. Create outbound referrals with specialty, reason, urgency, and prior auth information. Track status (Pending → Scheduled → Completed). Print the referral letter directly.

🤖 AI Features Prescriber Therapist

🎙️
Ambient AI Scribe

Records the provider-patient conversation and auto-drafts a SOAP note. Enable in Settings → AI Features → Ambient SOAP Generation. Start from any Encounters session.

🤖
AI Triage Chat

Patient-facing symptom checker. Patients describe symptoms and receive urgency guidance (Self-Care / Call Office / Go to ED). Located at Clinical Tools → AI Triage Chat.

🧠
AI Clinical Assistant (Floating Panel)

A persistent AI panel accessible from any screen. Ask clinical questions, check drug interactions, or request a differential diagnosis. Enable in Settings → AI Features → AI Clinical Assistant.

⚠️
CDS Alerts

Real-time clinical decision support alerts for drug-drug interactions, care gaps, and preventive care reminders. Located in Clinical Tools → CDS Alerts. Toggle in Settings.

Managing AI Features: All AI features can be individually enabled or disabled in Settings → AI Features. Use Enable All or Disable All for quick bulk changes.

🗣️ Voice Assistant Prescriber Therapist

Press Ctrl+Shift+V to activate voice commands. Say things like "Open chart for James Anderson", "Navigate to billing", or "Start telehealth session". Enable in Settings → AI Features → Voice Assistant.

Billing & Revenue Cycle ManagementPage 8

💰 Billing Dashboard Front Desk Admin

The Billing Dashboard provides an overview of outstanding AR, recent payments, claim statuses, and revenue trends. Navigate to Billing → Billing Dashboard.

📊 AR Summary

Total accounts receivable broken down by 0–30, 31–60, 61–90, and 90+ days.

✅ Clean Claim Rate

Percentage of claims submitted without errors. Target > 95%.

🔄 Claims in Process

Pending, submitted, and denied claim counts with one-click drill-down.

⚠️ Denials

Open denials requiring action. Click to open Denial Management.

📋 Claims Management Front Desk Admin

1
Create Claim

Claims are auto-generated from completed encounters. Go to Billing → Claims to review.

2
Verify & Scrub

Clarity runs automated claim edits. Red flags indicate missing information (diagnosis, NPI, authorization number).

3
Submit to Payer

Submit individually or use Batch Claims to submit multiple claims at once to a clearinghouse.

4
Track & Post Payments

ERA/EOB remittances post automatically. Use Charge Posting for manual adjustments.

Batch Submission: Use Billing → Batch Claims to select multiple claims, choose a clearinghouse, and submit in one action. Use Submit & Print to generate a batch report automatically.

✅ Insurance Eligibility Verification Front Desk

Navigate to Billing → Eligibility. Enter patient name and date of service. Clarity queries the payer's real-time eligibility API and returns copay, deductible, and coverage details. Run eligibility checks the day before an appointment for best results.

🚫 Denial Management Admin

Go to Billing → Denial Management. Each denial shows the payer reason code, claim details, and recommended action. You can resubmit directly from this screen with corrections, or file a formal appeal with supporting documentation.

📄 Patient Statements & Payment Plans Front Desk

Sending Statements

Go to Billing → Patient Statements. Filter by balance due or overdue date. Select statements and click Send Statement (email or print). Patients can pay online through the Patient Portal.

Payment Plans

Click a statement → click 📅 Setup Payment Plan. Select the number of monthly installments (2–12 months). The system calculates the monthly amount and schedules the plan. Patients are notified automatically.

🪪 Insurance Card Capture Front Desk

Navigate to Administration → Insurance Cards. Use Upload from Camera or Select File to capture insurance card images. Clarity uses OCR to extract and auto-fill member ID, group number, and payer name.

Interoperability · Marketing · AnalyticsPage 9

🌐 Care Everywhere — HIE Interoperability Prescriber Admin

Care Everywhere connects Clarity to external health information exchanges for real-time patient record access.

1
Navigate to Care Everywhere

Go to Clinical Tools → Care Everywhere (HIE).

2
Query External Records

Enter patient name and DOB. Select a network (CommonWell, Carequality, TEFCA, or State HIE). Click Query.

3
Review & Import

Results display external records (medications, labs, encounters). Click Import to Chart to pull selected data into Clarity.

42 CFR Part 2: Substance use treatment records require explicit patient consent before querying or sharing through the HIE. Clarity enforces this automatically.

📢 Practice Marketing & Reputation Admin

⭐ Reputation Management

View and respond to Google, Healthgrades, and Zocdoc reviews. Use AI-generated reply suggestions. Track average star rating over time.

📱 Social Media Planner

Draft and schedule posts for Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X. View calendar of planned content.

🔍 SEO & Directories

Monitor your practice's listing status across 12 major healthcare directories. Get alerts for outdated information.

Navigate to Administration → Marketing & Reputation.

📊 Analytics & Reporting Admin Prescriber

📈 Clinical Analytics

Patient demographics, appointment completion rates, no-show trends, medication counts, lab result volumes. Navigate to Reporting → Analytics.

⚡ Operational Signals (AI)

12 AI-powered performance indicators: scheduling efficiency, billing cycle length, care gap rates, and more. Navigate to Reporting → Operational Signals.

🎯 Care Gaps

Identifies patients overdue for preventive screenings, chronic disease management, or follow-up visits. Generate outreach lists. Navigate to Reporting → Care Gaps.

📋 Report Builder

Build custom reports by selecting data fields, date ranges, and filters. Export to CSV or print. Navigate to Reporting → Report Builder.

📬 Clinical Inbox All Roles

The Inbox (Navigation → Clinical Inbox) consolidates all incoming messages: lab results, refill requests, patient messages, prior auth updates, and task assignments. Filter by type, date, or sender. Mark items as read, action them, or forward to another team member.

💬 Staff Messaging All Roles

Secure internal messaging is available under Navigation → Staff Messaging. Messages are HIPAA-compliant and can include patient references, file attachments, and priority flags. All messages are audit-logged.

🔒 Security & Compliance Admin

Break-the-Glass (BTG)

Certain sensitive records are BTG-protected. Accessing them requires you to provide a documented clinical reason. All BTG accesses are permanently logged and visible to compliance officers under Administration → BTG Audit Log.

Note: Inappropriate BTG access is a HIPAA violation and may result in disciplinary action.

Audit Trail

Every action in Clarity — chart views, edits, prescriptions, logins — is logged with user, timestamp, and IP address. Access the full log under Administration → Audit Trail. Admins and Front Desk supervisors can filter, export, and review logs.
