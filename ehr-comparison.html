🔑 Key Takeaways

  • Clarity EHR (100%) — Matches or exceeds Epic on every category including CareEverywhere (HIE). Uniquely combines psychiatric specialization with full-spectrum practice management, marketing, and AI-powered features.
  • Epic (95%) — Remains the gold standard for large health systems and interoperability, but lacks practice marketing tools and has no go-to-session quick-launch telehealth feature.
  • athenaOne (85%) — Excels at revenue cycle management and billing, but falls behind on telehealth capabilities (no group sessions, screen share), patient portal languages, and documentation tools (no sticky notes).
  • Tebra (60%) — Strong in marketing/reputation management and core billing, but has zero interoperability (no HIE, no FHIR, no CareEverywhere), limited clinical depth, and no psychiatric-specific features.
  • Mend (25%) — Telehealth-only platform with no EHR capabilities, no billing/RCM, no prescribing, no interoperability, and no clinical documentation. Only suitable as a supplemental telehealth add-on.
