# Job Description: SOC 2 Compliance Lead / CISO
## Full-Time, Dedicated Role

**Position Type:** Full-Time (0.5 FTE minimum, 1.0 FTE ideal)  
**Reports To:** CEO or CTO  
**Duration:** 18 months (Jun 2026–Dec 2027)  
**Salary Range:** $80K–$130K (depending on experience + FTE)  
**Start Date:** [Q3 2026]  

---

## Position Overview

Clarity EHR is pursuing SOC 2 Type II certification to unlock enterprise healthcare customers. The SOC 2 Compliance Lead manages evidence collection, policy documentation, control testing, and coordination with external auditors. This is the single point of accountability for achieving certification on time and budget.

**Key Success:** Deliver SOC 2 Type II report by December 31, 2027 with 0–2 minor findings.

---

## Primary Responsibilities

### 1. Program Management (30%)

- Own the 18-month SOC 2 program timeline and milestones
- Track progress weekly (schedule slip, budget variance, resource gaps)
- Escalate blockers to CEO/CTO within 24 hours
- Prepare monthly compliance dashboard for leadership
- Manage readiness assessment and auditor RFP process
- Coordinate between engineering, ops, finance, and auditor teams

**Skills Needed:** Project management, executive communication, timeline tracking, risk escalation

---

### 2. Policy Documentation & Governance (25%)

- Author and maintain SOC 2 policies:
  - Security policy (framework, ownership, incident response)
  - Access control policy (RBAC, provisioning/deprovisioning)
  - Data classification & confidentiality policy
  - Change management procedures
  - Risk management procedures
  - Backup & disaster recovery plan
  - Key rotation procedures
  - Vendor risk assessment procedures

- Update policies quarterly as controls evolve
- Ensure all policies are accessible to staff
- Obtain CEO/CTO sign-off before auditor review

**Skills Needed:** Policy writing, regulatory knowledge (HIPAA), compliance frameworks, technical understanding

---

### 3. Evidence Collection & Management (25%)

- Set up automated evidence collection systems:
  - Audit log exports (monthly)
  - Screenshots of controls (monitoring dashboard, access reviews, etc.)
  - Test result logs (backup restoration, failover drills, etc.)
  - Incident investigation reports

- Organize evidence in compliance folder with clear labeling
- Maintain evidence index/spreadsheet (control → evidence mapping)
- Review evidence monthly for completeness
- Investigate and document any control failures
- Prepare evidence package for auditor

**Skills Needed:** Attention to detail, data organization, technical logging knowledge, evidence management

---

### 4. Control Implementation & Testing (15%)

- Work with engineering to ensure controls operate as designed:
  - Validate CSRF protection on all POST/PUT/DELETE
  - Verify audit log immutability (test DELETE/UPDATE prevention)
  - Check encryption key rotation procedures
  - Confirm rate limiting thresholds
  - Test MFA/elevation workflows

- Schedule & coordinate quarterly testing:
  - Backup restoration drill (test RTO/RPO)
  - Failover test (verify disaster recovery)
  - Access control review (verify RBAC is correct)
  - Policy compliance check (all staff aware + trained)

- Document all test results with date, participants, outcome

**Skills Needed:** Technical testing, security knowledge, procedure documentation, reliability

---

### 5. Auditor Coordination (5%)

- Serve as primary contact for external auditor
- Respond to auditor information requests within 24 hours
- Schedule auditor interviews (with CTO, ops, finance leads)
- Provide evidence on-demand throughout audit period
- Resolve any findings or exceptions with auditor

**Skills Needed:** Communication, responsiveness, problem-solving, vendor management

## Day-to-Day Activities

### Weekly
- 1-hour progress meeting with CEO/CTO
- Review evidence collection (automated or manual)
- Follow up on any outstanding items from auditor/team

### Monthly
- Prepare compliance dashboard (status, metrics, risks)
- Review all evidence collected that month
- Hold all-staff compliance check-in (30 min) — remind of policies, upcoming tests
- Send compliance email to team summarizing progress

### Quarterly
- Conduct control testing (backup restore, failover, access review)
- Update all policies with changes made in prior quarter
- Prepare detailed status report for board/investors (if applicable)

### As-Needed
- Respond to auditor questions (within 24 hours)
- Investigate incident or control failure
- Resolve blockers (escalate to CEO/CTO if needed)
- Add new controls or update procedures if customer requirements change

## Questions from Candidates

**Q: What if the audit fails?**
A: It won't if controls are operating properly. Our job is to make sure they do.

**Q: Is this a permanent role?**
A: No—18-month project. After report delivery, role transitions to "Compliance Manager" (maintenance, 10–15 hrs/week).

**Q: Do I need healthcare background?**
A: Helpful but not required. Compliance skills matter more.

**Q: What if I disagree with a control?**
A: Escalate to CTO/CEO. Auditor makes final call. Your job is to document and test, not design.
