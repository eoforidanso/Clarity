🔑 Key Takeaways

  • Clarity EHR (100%) — The only platform built natively for outpatient behavioral health with full-spectrum coverage: EHR + billing + telehealth + HIE + marketing + AI in one product. PHQ-9, treatment plans, and 42 CFR Part 2 are native, not add-ons. Fastest onboarding (<1 hour) and lowest cognitive load of the three.
  • athenaOne (85%) — Strongest revenue cycle management and billing engine of the three. Good interoperability via CommonWell. Falls behind on telehealth (no group video, no screen share, no one-click launch), documentation tools (no smart phrases), and psychiatric specialization requires costly add-ons.
  • Tebra (60%) — Strongest practice marketing and reputation management tools. Good for small primary care practices. Critical gaps: zero interoperability (no HIE, no FHIR R4, no CareEverywhere), no psychiatric features, no BTG audit log, no smart phrases. Not suitable for behavioral health compliance requirements.
