## 1. Executive Summary

Clarity EHR is pursuing **SOC 2 Type II Certification** to demonstrate industry-standard security, availability, and confidentiality controls to enterprise healthcare customers.

**Business Case:**
- **Current State:** Healthcare customers ask for SOC 2; we don't have it
- **Target State:** SOC 2 Type II report in hand by Q4 2027
- **Expected Outcome:** Unlock enterprise deals, reduce customer RFP friction, build trust
- **Investment:** ~$150–190K (staff + auditor) over 18 months

**Success Metrics:**
- ✅ SOC 2 Type II report issued by year-end 2027
- ✅ <4 material weaknesses identified
- ✅ All critical controls operating effectively
- ✅ Zero audit delays due to missing evidence

## 2. Program Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goals

1. **Achieve SOC 2 Type II Certification** in Security, Availability, and Confidentiality
2. **Demonstrate Sustained Control Effectiveness** over 12-month period (Sep 2026–Sep 2027)
3. **Close Compliance Gaps** identified in readiness assessment
4. **Establish Compliance Culture** — embed controls into operational processes

### Key Objectives

| Objective | Timeline | Owner | Success Criteria |

| Readiness assessment complete | Q3 2026 | Security Lead | Gap analysis delivered, scored |

| Auditor selected & engaged | Q4 2026 | CEO + Finance | NDA signed, audit plan approved |
| Evidence period begins | Q4 2026 (Sep 1) | All | Baseline established |

## 3. Program Scope

### In Scope

✅ **Clarity EHR Platform** — API, database, authentication, audit logging, monitoring  
✅ **Three Trust Service Criteria** — Security (CC), Availability (A), Confidentiality (C)  
✅ **12-Month Evidence Period** — Sep 1, 2026 to Sep 1, 2027  
✅ **Type II Certification** — Controls tested over time, not snapshot

### Out of Scope

❌ **Infrastructure** — Managed by cloud provider (Digital Ocean, AWS, Cloudflare)  
❌ **Processing Integrity (PI)** — Lower priority, can add in future cycles  
❌ **Privacy (P)** — Handled by GDPR/CCPA separately  
❌ **Mobile Apps** — Separate scope if audited later  

**Full Details:** See `SOC2_SCOPE_DEFINITION.md`

## 5. Program Phases & Milestones

### Phase 1: Preparation (Q3 2026)

**Duration:** 8 weeks  
**Effort:** 120–150 hours  
**Owner:** CISO + CTO

| Week | Milestone | Deliverable |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| 1–2 | Scope finalization | Scope definition approved + signed off |
| 3–4 | Readiness assessment | Gap analysis + remediation plan |
| 5–6 | Policy documentation | Security policy, risk register, procedures |
| 7–8 | Auditor selection | SOW signed, audit plan approved |

**Go/No-Go Decision:** Gap count <10, timeline feasible, budget approved

---

### Phase 2: Implementation & Evidence Setup (Q4 2026)

**Duration:** 12 weeks  
**Effort:** 80–100 hours  
**Owner:** CTO + Ops + CISO

| Month | Milestone | Deliverable |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Oct | Control implementation | Policies operationalized, monitoring live |
| Oct | Evidence systems | Automated log collection, screenshot tools |
| Nov | Testing | Failover drills, backup recovery tests |
| Dec | Evidence baseline | Baseline controls established, month 1 evidence collected |

**Go/No-Go Decision:** All critical controls operational, evidence collection automated

---

### Phase 3: Evidence Collection (Jan 2027–Sep 2027)

**Duration:** 9 months  
**Effort:** 10–15 hours/month  
**Owner:** CISO + Security Team

**Monthly Activities:**
- ✅ Collect audit logs, screenshots, test results
- ✅ Review evidence completeness
- ✅ Investigate anomalies, document resolutions
- ✅ Report to leadership on compliance status

**Quarterly Activities:**
- ✅ Access control review
- ✅ Risk register update
- ✅ Policy compliance testing
- ✅ Incident summary review

---

### Phase 4: Audit Execution (Oct 2027–Dec 2027)

**Duration:** 12 weeks  
**Effort:** 20–30 hours/week (high interaction period)  
**Owner:** All teams (with auditor)

| Month | Activity | Responsibility |
|-------|----------|-----------------|
| Oct | Interim audit | Auditor reviews 9 months of evidence |
| Nov | Final audit | Auditor conducts fieldwork, interviews |
| Dec | Report issuance | Auditor finalizes + delivers report |

**Expected Outcome:** SOC 2 Type II report with 0–2 minor findings

## 7. Risk Management

### Key Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Timeline Slip** | Medium | High | Dedicated compliance lead, monthly tracking |
| **Key Person Leave** | Low | High | Document procedures, cross-train 2+ people |
| **Evidence Gaps** | Medium | Medium | Automated collection, monthly reviews |
| **Budget Overrun** | Low | Medium | Detailed budget, vendor quotes, scope control |
| **Auditor Issue** | Low | High | Vendor selection process, reference checks |
| **Control Failure** | Low | High | Monthly testing, incident response drills |

### Risk Escalation

- **Red (Critical):** CEO + board notification within 24 hours
- **Yellow (High):** CEO + sponsor notification within 48 hours
- **Green (Medium):** CISO manages within scope, inform sponsor monthly
